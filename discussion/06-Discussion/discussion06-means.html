Data

The following code reads in the Madison weather data and calculates the average July temperature for each year. Treat the years from 1869 to 1898 as early Madison and from 1991 to 2020 as recent Madison.

## Read and transform the Madison weather data
mw = read_csv("../../data/madison-weather-2020-clean-2.csv")

mw_july = mw %>%
  filter(month=="Jul") %>%
  filter(year < 1899 | year > 1990) %>%
  group_by(year) %>%
  summarize(tavg = mean(tavg)) %>%
  mutate(era = case_when(
    year < 1900 ~ "early",
    year > 1989 ~ "recent"))
